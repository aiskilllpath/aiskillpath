## 🚀 About

**AISkillPath** is a curated AI learning platform that helps beginners 
and aspiring AI professionals learn artificial intelligence the smart way. 
Instead of wasting hours searching for scattered resources, learners get 
organized roadmaps, handpicked free courses, summary notes, prompt templates, 
project guides, and mentorship support — all in one place.

---

## 🎯 What We Offer

- ✅ **Curated Learning Roadmaps** — Step-by-step paths from beginner to advanced
- ✅ **Best Free Courses** — Handpicked from MIT, Stanford, Google, Coursera, and more
- ✅ **Summary Notes** — Concise notes for every topic for faster learning
- ✅ **Prompt Templates** — 200+ ready-to-use prompts for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini
- ✅ **Project Guides** — Real-world AI projects to build your portfolio
- ✅ **Career Resources** — Resume templates, interview prep, job search strategy
- ✅ **Mentorship Support** — Personal guidance and doubt clearing
