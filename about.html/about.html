About AISkillPath

We believe AI education should be accessible, affordable, and structured for everyone.

Our Story

When we started learning AI, we faced the same problem everyone faces — thousands of courses, no clear roadmap, and expensive programs that most students can't afford.

We spent months searching, filtering, and organizing the best free AI resources from top universities and platforms. We created structured learning paths, wrote summary notes, built project guides, and developed prompt templates.

Then we realized — if we struggled this much, others are struggling too. That's when AISkillPath was born.

Our mission is simple: save you hundreds of hours of searching and give you a clear, structured path to learn AI — affordably.
